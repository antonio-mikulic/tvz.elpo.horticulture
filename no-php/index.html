About us

Who are we?

At Greenwhite Horticulture we believe in quality, beauty and speed. We have been in this business for over 25 years and each of our 63 employees has more than 10 years of experience. Together we have worked on over 9000 projects, most of them being personal gardens but we landscaped an entire city once. Our headquarter is in Zagreb, Croatia, but we have offices in Split, Rijeka and Osijek.

Our work can be seen all over Croatia because no matter where you are, we will find at least one of our employees to make your garden perfect for you. They can also teach you how to take care of your garden, which pesticide to use, what vegetables, fruits and plants you should plant-based on your soil, climate and many other factors and answer every other question you have.

Our Services

What do we offer?

Planning a landscape

Interested in improving the looks of your courtyard? This is the service you want to get! Work with our team of experts to make your garden perfect for you and your needs. After calling us, we will send someone to the site, make a 3D model of your garden and after listening to your requests make planned layout of your future garden.

Prices depend on size of your garden as well as how many adjustments you want to make. Base price is 1000 kunas per day.

Building a landscape

You have a planned landscape but can't build it yourself? This is where we come in! Our team of experts will build your garden for you! We suit our needs for you, so we will only come when you want us to, that includes working nights and weekends. Our employees are swift and careful, so your garden will be ready in no time.

Prices depend on how much workers you need and when are they working. Base price is 75 kunas per worker per hour. 25% increase during weekends and 50% during night.

Maintaining a landscape

Don't feel like shovelling snow in winter, mowing your grass during summer or doing any work in your garden at all? Call us and we will do it for you, as long as it takes place in your garden!

Prices depend on how much workers you need and when are they working. Base price is 75 kunas per worker per hour. 25% increase during weekends and 50% during night.
